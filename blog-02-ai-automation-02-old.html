AI and automation in my projects

Blog 2 • AI & Automation • Full details with example logic and video.

Here I note how I use simple AI models or automation scripts together with robotics. This can be object detection, distance checking, or just automating data logging so I do not have to manually watch every sensor reading.

The idea is: robots read the environment, some logic or AI model decides what to do, and then the motors or actuators move. Even very simple “if/else” logic is a kind of automation.

# sample AI / automation style pseudo-code

distance = read_sensor()

if distance < 20:
    stop_robot()
    print("Obstacle detected")
else:
    move_forward()
